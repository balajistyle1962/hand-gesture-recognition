About Us

We are three friends Balaji N R, Aravindh Raj N, Vallathuru Niranjan from Velammal Engineering College, who teamed up for their Final Year Project i.e Capstone to create and application that can truly help the people in need. We set out to find new developments in LSTM. We have trained the dataset for measuring the gesture i.e. the hand gesture, So that we could able to understand the gesture. We harnessed medipipes capabilities to create a live gesture capturing for ASL characters and phrases to work with low processing power plaftorms like mobile phones. We created out own datasets for this approach and then finally created an android app to help people even learn ASL along with out new Live Gesture translations to practice ASL. We hope this helps hearing impared people in their lives in someway.
